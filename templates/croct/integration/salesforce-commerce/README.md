# Introduction

Want to turn your **Salesforce Commerce store activity** into **personalized experiences** with no extra development
work?

This integration connects your [Salesforce Commerce](https://www.salesforce.com/products/commerce-cloud/) store to
Croct, automatically tracking store events such as product views, cart changes, checkouts, and orders, and enriching
every user profile with Salesforce Commerce customer data so marketers can build audiences, run AB tests, and
personalize the storefront.

Follow [this tutorial](https://docs.croct.com/immersion/tutorials/salesforce-commerce-integration) to bring your 
Salesforce Commerce store data into Croct and get started.
